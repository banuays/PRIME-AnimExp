Process Model Comprehension Experiment

Process Model Comprehension Experiment

This study is conducted by Dr. Banu Aysolmaz and Prof. Dr. Hajo Reijers, Business Informatics Group, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam as part of PRIME project funded by EU H2020-MSCA-IF-2014 call. The purpose of this experiment is to investigate how process model animation affects the comprehension by model readers.

General information on the study is at this moment provided verbally. Please consult the instructors if you have any questions.
